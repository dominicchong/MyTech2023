Kickstart your tech career with our help

We are CS students. We want to help our friends in tech to kickstart our tech career.This program is inclusively designed for everybody in tech, especially university students and fresh graduates looking for internship/full-time opportunity.

We aim to build an effective talent pipeline from universities to the workplace. This career fair connects students to their future by experiencing the real touch of tech industry - what's happening, what do the bosses expect, how to become a great engineer.
